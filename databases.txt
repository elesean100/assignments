What is a Database? 
A program designed to hold data for users.

Why is an Database necessary? 
Allows for more storage space for users.

What can be stored in a Database? 
Usernames, Passwords, Emails. Anyhting saved on a computer.

What types of Databases are there? 
Users, graphs.

different jobs require different Databases.

What is a Query?

What is Encryption? 
a security protocol for databases to protect information

Why is it important at the Database level? 
protect information such as identity.

What is Object Permanence? 
Object Permanence is when certain parts of the data
are remembered even when hidden.
